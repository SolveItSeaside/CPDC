Business Issues
01
Issues
The top of the hierarchy. A Business Issue is the overall purpose behind a set of customer actions — the "why". Everything in the taxonomy sits underneath an Issue.
Reveal Issues
Groups
02
Groups
Groups sit inside Issues and organise actions into product or service categories. A Group always belongs to exactly one Issue. Engagement policies set at Group level apply to every action within it.
Reveal Groups
Actions
03
Actions
Actions are the individual offers or messages CDH presents to each customer. Each Action belongs to exactly one Issue and one Group. In U+ Bank, Actions under Credit Cards are renamed "Offers" — a key exam point.
Reveal Actions
Treatments
04
Treatments
A Treatment is the channel-specific version of an Action — how it looks and feels per channel. One Action can have multiple Treatments. Treatments hold the creative: images, copy, dynamic placeholders.
Reveal Treatments
Channels
05
Channels
Channels are how CDH delivers Actions and Treatments to customers — inbound (customer initiates) or outbound (bank proactively reaches out). Configured in NBA Designer under the Channels tab.
